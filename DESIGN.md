# Design Guidelines

## Colors
- Primary: #e11d48 (rose-600) — CTAs, highlights, best-price badges
- Secondary: #0f172a (slate-950) — backgrounds, dark surfaces
- Accent: #e11d48
- Background: #ffffff
- Foreground: #0f172a
- Muted: #f8fafc
- Muted Foreground: #64748b
- Border: #e2e8f0
- Card: #ffffff
- Card Foreground: #0f172a
- Navbar: #0f172a (dark, editorial)
- Sidebar: #0f172a

## Typography
- Heading Font: Playfair Display — editorial, fashion-forward
- Body Font: DM Sans — clean, modern, highly readable

## Elevation & Surfaces
- Cards with subtle shadow, clean white backgrounds
- Dark navbar contrasts with light content area
- Best-price badge uses primary rose color prominently

## Component Style
- Rounded corners (rounded-xl for cards)
- Minimal borders, rely on shadow for depth
- Price comparison cards: platform logo + price + "Best Price" badge when applicable
- Search bar: large, prominent, centered hero layout
- Clean grid layout for results
