The Gap in the Market

Fashion pricing is broken
for professionals.

The Problem

Fashion enthusiasts, stylists, and industry workers must visit every platform individually — Zara, ASOS, Shein, H&M — then manually collate prices into a spreadsheet. Hours of work, constant tab-switching, and still no guarantee they found the best price.

The Solution

One search surfaces prices from every major fashion platform side by side — instantly. No spreadsheets, no tab-hopping. Fashion professionals get the cross-platform price intel they need in seconds, not hours.
