# FashionPrice — Fashion Price Comparison App

## What It Is
Web app for fashion enthusiasts, stylists, and fashion industry workers to search any fashion item and instantly see prices from multiple platforms side by side.

## Target Users
- Fashion enthusiasts
- Stylists
- Fashion industry workers

## Core Problem
Users currently visit each platform manually and build spreadsheets to compare prices. This app eliminates that entirely.

## Hero Feature: Best Price Finder
Search once → best price highlighted at top + full side-by-side comparison across platforms (Zara, ASOS, Shein, H&M, and more).

## MVP Features
- Fashion item search
- Multi-platform price comparison (Zara, ASOS, Shein, H&M, etc.)
- Best price highlighted prominently at top of results
- Side-by-side platform comparison table/grid

## Brand Tone
Clean, editorial, professional. Feels like a premium fashion publication, not a discount aggregator.
